# Co-one Group Knowledge Base

## Overview
Co-one Group builds robust digital infrastructures enabling customers to excel in their worlds. They focus on reliability, performance, and strategic network architecture. They operate in 4 main domains:
1. **Network** - SD-WAN and enterprise networking
2. **Cloud** - Azure, multi-cloud, and datacenter
3. **Infra** - On-premise and edge infrastructure
4. **WiFi & Workplace** - Enterprise wireless solutions and modern workplace environments

## The 4 Companies
- **DataMobility**: Consultants translating customer needs into robust network/infrastructure architectures. Focus on strategy, cloud, data, and network automation for healthcare, retail, and public sectors.
- **iunxi**: Specialists in managing secure networks. Offers co-managed to fully managed services (Network as a Service). Focus on continuity, security (ISO 27001, NEN 7510), and performance.
- **Infield ICT**: Cloud infrastructure experts with a strong track record in hospitals/healthcare. Deep knowledge of Nutanix technology for secure, scalable, future-proof platforms. Azure Cloud Experts.
- **Veducon**: High-end WiFi and network environments specialist. Proven track record in Cisco technology and retail environments where scalable and secure connectivity is crucial. Also focuses on Security, Automation, Cameras, and IoT.

## Key Offerings & Methodologies
- **co-discover, co-create, co-deliver, co-manage**: End-to-end lifecycle management.
- **Target Industries**: Healthcare (hospitals, care institutions), Retail (stores, chains), Manufacturing, Logistics (warehouses), Education.
- **Technologies**: Cisco, Nutanix, Azure, Meraki, Fortinet, Juniper, Ruckus.
